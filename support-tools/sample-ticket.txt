--- SUBJECT ---
Booking confirmation page fails to load after payment – users stuck in a redirect loop

--- DESCRIPTION ---
Since approximately 10:45 AM UTC today, multiple users under the Atoll Resorts Group enterprise account have reported that after entering payment details and clicking "Confirm Booking", they are redirected to a blank white page. The browser URL changes to /confirmation?ref= but the reference number is empty and the page content never loads. Some users have reported the page enters a redirect loop between /checkout and /confirmation before eventually timing out with a generic browser error.

This issue is affecting all three of our properties: Atoll Maldives, Atoll Bali, and Atoll Phuket.

We have confirmed this does NOT occur on Firefox but IS reproducible on Chrome (latest) and Edge.

Affected users: 47 confirmed, likely more.
Revenue impact: Estimated USD $92,000 in lost or incomplete bookings.

--- STEPS TO REPRODUCE ---
1. Navigate to https://app.hospitality-platform.com
2. Search for any hotel with a check-in date of 2026-09-01 or later.
3. Select any available room and proceed to checkout.
4. Fill in guest details and payment information using any valid card (Visa 4111 1111 1111 1111 tested).
5. Click "Confirm Booking".
6. Observe: Browser URL changes to /confirmation?ref= (no reference ID appended).
7. Observe: Page displays blank white screen or enters a redirect loop.

--- OBSERVED BEHAVIOR ---
- URL becomes /confirmation?ref= with an empty `ref` parameter.
- Page content does not render.
- Chrome DevTools Network tab shows a 302 redirect from /confirmation back to /checkout repeatedly.
- JavaScript console shows: "Uncaught TypeError: Cannot read properties of undefined (reading 'bookingReference')"

--- EXPECTED BEHAVIOR ---
- URL should be /confirmation?ref=<BOOKING_REFERENCE_ID>
- Confirmation page should render with booking details, guest name, check-in/out dates, and a receipt download button.

--- ADDITIONAL CONTEXT ---

- No issue found with bookings placed before the deployment window.
- Our internal test account (qa.automation@hospitality-platform.com) can also reproduce this.
- We attempted clearing cache, incognito mode, and a different network – all result in the same behavior on Chrome/Edge.
